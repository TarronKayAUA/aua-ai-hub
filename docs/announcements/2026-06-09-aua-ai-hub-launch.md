# Welcome to the AUA AI Hub

*June 9, 2026*

The AUA AI Hub is now live. This site gives the American University of Antigua College of Medicine community a single, reliable place for artificial intelligence (AI) reference material, reviewed tools, learning resources, and conference planning.

What is here today:

- [AI Basics](../basics/how-llms-work.md): a plain-language primer on how large language models work, a full glossary, and a page addressing common misconceptions.
- [Tools](../tools/index.md): a directory of AI tools with governance statuses. All statuses are provisional pending AI Governance Committee ratification.
- [Learning](../learning/index.md): curated learning paths for faculty and students, from first steps to technical depth.
- [Conferences](../conferences.md): a calendar of AI and medical education meetings with verified dates and deadlines.

What is coming next: an automated news section covering general AI developments, medical education research, and clinical practice, refreshed nightly, plus a weekly digest delivered by email.

Questions, corrections, and suggestions are welcome; contact information is on the [About page](../about.md).
